I am well acquainted with the design process, from concept ideation to physically and digitally building and rendering models. When working with physical models, I often work with pink foam, sign foam, and acrylic. When modeling digitally, I use SolidWorks to build and and Keyshot to render. The Adobe Suite is my weapon of choice, but I am quickly learning HTML and CSS, and would call these my main passion. I currently just create webpages from scratch with a text editing program so that I can learn the nuisances of the coding, but am more than eager to learn other methods. My HTML/web design work can be found in my portfolio.

EMPLOYERS ONLY LIKE GUYS WITH SKILLS

Most of my experience with the Adobe Creative Suite is in Photoshop, but I also frequently work in Illustrator, Fireworks, and InDesign, so I am well versed in those programs as well. I often use CS6 to edit photos, create graphic layouts, and make renderings, but I also frequently use it to mock up web pages, email templates and email designs. Samples of this work can be found in my portfolio as well. In addition, I also have some light experience in Dreamweaver and Flash.

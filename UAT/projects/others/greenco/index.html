THE OLD SITE

Ward Green, the owner of Green Commercial Properties was in need of a website update, and was referred to me by my boss at Phoenix Direct. Naturally, I took this on as my second freelance web design job. His site had not been updated since the late 90â€™s, and he was in desperate need of a new, more up-to-date site with a much more modern look and feel.

REDESIGNING & REBUILDING

I talked with Ward extensively about what he wanted out of his new site, what he wanted to keep and what he wanted to change. He provided me with current info and copy, and I went out and took background photography and video to use on the site, in order to give it a more modern feel, incorporating changes along the way.

The Green Commercial Properties site was my second freelance web design project. As with the Dollar Concrete site, I used the web-oriented coding program, Brackets, to code this site from scratch. I had learned a lot with the Dollar project, and wanted to apply what I had learned to this project, as well as try out some new and different things.

THE NEW SITE

The new site incorporates the feedback that I had received from Ward throughout the project, all packaged up with new styling and up-to-date information. In August, we set a deadline of finishing the site by the end of 2015, which we were able to meet, and the site went online shortly after. I will be working with Green Commercial Properties to keep the site updated as new content comes along.

MODULES

SMART SEARCH

What we coined as "smart search" was the first of two modules that I designed to go into a revamped navigation bar across all of our sites. The module contains a type-ahead search functionality whose results can either be applied to your specific vehicle or all vehicles, and also features space for returning several products or product types that are related to your current search.

MY GARAGE

The second of those two modules was a "my garage" feature that allowed users to save selected vehicles for easy access later. Vehicles can be added or removed and even renamed, a feature targeted more towards our wholesale customers that have shops full of cars they need to keep straight. The design process for these modules also focused more heavily on mobile, and thus helped standardize the previously sub-par search and vehicle selection processes on mobile devices.

PRODUCT TAGS

This widget initially arose as part of implementing a Prop 65 warning alert. In designing this tag, it was brought up that a similar structure could be used beyond this one alert, out of which "product tags" was created. I helped create a library of warning, alert, status, and promotion tags that would show up under different circumstances, as well as defined icons, styling, and page locations for each one.

SEARCH RESULTS

In looking at all of the locations on our sites that displayed product results of some sort, and as we were looking to add new ones, I decided to design a more standardized search result container that could be used in multiple locations. The container also included various improvements we found through analyzing user behavior data, including making the call-to-actions more prominent, and adding fitment/supersession information to help users distinguish between similar parts for different vehicles.

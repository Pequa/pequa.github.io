Work Projects

Default Pages

Project Role: Director

The highly-branded default pages on SimplePart sites are a big selling point for our OEMs, due to the fact that they serve as the main "face" of the stie and need to tie in to the OEM's property. Because of this, most of the designs more bespoke than our "interior" platform pages, requiring extra attention from a design perspective to ensure that all of the platform components function on all screen sizes, while still maintaining the OEM's branding and CI.

Assembly Pages

Project Role: Director

The assembly page is the intermediary between a broad category page and a specific product page, displaying groups of products grouped into assemblies. Although this page doesn't recieve as much traffic as the product page, it displays high-density information in a table-like format due to the high number of potential parts that it can contain, and therefore especially requires that the information be displayed in a responsive format on smaller devices to ensure usability.

Product Pages

Project Role: Director & Designer

The product page is one of the most essential pages of the SimplePart platform, since most of our users land directly on a product page from the SERP. As such, it is essential that it be accessible on a wide range of devices and screen sizes, and have a consistent but optimized user experience between them. Additionally, since this is the page that a majority of users add products to their cart, conversion needs to be top of mind, no matter the viewport.

dollarconcrete.com

THE OLD SITE

Dollar Concrete Construcion is a privately-owned commercial concrete building contractor based out of Norcross, GA. Their existing website had not been updated since 2006 and contained outdated information, so their site was not driving much traffic. Dollar wanted to revamp the design to give themselves a better presence online.

REDESIGNING & REBUILDING

I met with the Dollar Concrete senior management team several times and discussed what changes they wanted in content, what kind of style they were looking for, and I worked with them on how best to lay it out. They provided updated images of current jobs, copy, and information that helped me build the design to suit their needs.

This project was the first full front-end web design job I had done, so in order to better familiarize myself with HTML and CSS along the way, I coded the site from scratch in the web-oriented coding program, Brackets. Because it was my first freelance project and I was still fairly new to coding, Dollar was very accomodating with their delivery dates, allowing me time to make sure everything was done right.

THE NEW SITE

After incorporating all of the elements discussed throughout the design process, I presented Dollar with a final local version of the site. After some finishing tweaks, the site was ready to upload and went live in summer of 2015. The new site incorporates more modernized interactive elements and up-to-date information put together in a newer, fresher style.

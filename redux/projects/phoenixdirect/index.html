EMAIL & WEB DESIGNS

Phoenix Direct is a direct-to-consumer fulfillment agency that allows companies to sell products directly to consumers through catalogs and the web. Their services include web design and email marketing, photogrphy and catalog design, and fulfillment services. I have worked in the web design department at Phoenix since 2013.

In my time in the web department, I have designed many emails for these clients whose needs range across a broad spectrum of styles and markets. Both the email and web graphic design processes involve constant feedback from the clients, incorporating their needs and desired changes throughout the process, and working on multiple projects at a time.

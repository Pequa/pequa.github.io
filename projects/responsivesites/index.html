RESPONSIVE SITES

UTILIZING THE PLATFORM

When our company built our third-generation platform, one of the driving factors was that it needed to be built on a bootstrap grid so it could be fully responsive and more accessible. We had almost a dozen manufacturers that needed to be ported over to the new platform, so in the interest of time, most of the sites that were ported over did not get a full responsive treatment at the start. They used the new platform, they just didn’t really make use of the new platform’s capabilities. Now that we’re making a second pass, Toyota is the first manufacturer that I “re-skinned” to make use of responsive design.

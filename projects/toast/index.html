TOAST

AN INDIVIDUAL & SOCIAL SOLUTION

The project problem was to create either a social product or a personal product that enhanced the wine drinking experience. But after ideating some potential solutions, I realized that the beauty of an app is that it can serve both of those purposes. What resulted was Toast.

THE WINE CHARM

For the social aspect of the app, I didn’t want to limit the app to social media, I wanted to encourage real-world interaction. I had the idea that in social environments, toasting wine glasses is a common practice. In addition, wine charms are also very common in social settings for differentiating wine and expressing individual style.

So I decided to base the app off of is this: if everyone were to have a "smart" wine charm that could sense when its parent wine glass was being toasted, the charms would be able to talk to each other and record who toasted with who, when, and where. Then, once two glasses were “toasted”, two options would be available: share the toast with friends (socially), or add the toast to your collection (personally).

THE SOCIAL EXPERIENCE

Once the toasts were logged, if you choose to share the toast friends, the app would take the information gathered from the toast and translate it into a Facebook status. This would give you the opportunity to share the experience that you’re having with friends, telling them what you are doing, where, and with whom.

THE PERSONAL EXPERIENCE

Say, however, you did not want to share the event with friends, but you wanted to remember what kind of wine you were drinking and how you liked it. Then, you could use Toast to take a picture of the label, from which it would log the wine name, vintage year, and when you had it. The app would then prompt you for a rating, and log that along with the wine information to create a unique, personal wine history.

DATA IS BEAUTIFUL

With all the information about the wine stored, as well as your opinion on it, the app would then able to show you data about your preferences on wine, and then make recommendations on other wines you might like. This information would help you become in tune to your preferences, and help guide you towards wine that is best suited for you.

BRINGING IT ALL TOGETHER

Above is a map of the functions of Toast, showing how both the social and personal solutions are integrated into the functions of the app. When a toast is sensed, you can choose to share the Who, What and Where of the toast with the world, save the information about the wine to your log for recommendations, or both.

TOAST

The app received positive feedback at the final presentation of the mini-project, including positive feedback from a wine enthusiast and business owner, who said that an app like this would definitely be appreciated in the wine drinking community. Personally, I was very pleased with how it turned out, and would love to actually build Toast one day.

OEM SITES

INFINITI USA

The design of the default page of our manufacturer sites is one of our biggest selling tools to our OEMs. It gives us, and them, an opportunity to expand their branding into the online parts e-commerce space, it helps market our product to their dealers, and helps their dealers market to their customers. Much of our traffic is brought in through organic search results on other pages of the sites, but the default page serves as a marketing backbone for the site as a whole.

MINI CANADA

While the design of the site needs to be brand-compliant and match the look and feel of the manufacturer, our platform still has fixed functionality that needs to be built around. Maintaining the brand’s guidelines and layouts while working within the scope of our platform’s abilities often involves lots of iteration and a very fine balance, but is very much a fun and fulfilling challenge.

VOLVO USA

Some OEMs are much stricter around brand guidelines than others, with different amounts of room for flexibility depending on the brand. Each default page design that we do requires a lot of back and forth with the client about design, layout, logo usage, and often functionality in general, which often involves approaching each design differently.

SUBARU OF AMERICA

With every new OEM site or redesign that we go through, platform changes have likely been made since the last design, or new ones are being introduced. This means that all of our designs are ever-evolving, often necessitating taking into account not only planning room for current features, but also designing in a way that new features, modules, or styling can be easily injected or replaced in the future without extensive effort.

VOLKSWAGEN USA

In addition to designing around brand guidelines and platform restrictions, we also have to take into account content and SEO. A good portion of our traffic comes in from organic search, so putting the right content with the right keywords in the right place can have great implications on our sites. Thus, our design team works closely not only with development, but with the marketing and content teams as well.

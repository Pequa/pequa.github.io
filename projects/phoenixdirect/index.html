PHOENIX DIRECT


Phoenix Direct is a direct-to-consumer fulfillment agency that allows companies to sell products directly to consumers through catalogs and the web. Their services include web design, web/email marketing, web development, photography, catalog design, and fulfillment services. I have worked in the web design department at Phoenix since 2013.

EMAIL & WEB DESIGNS


At Phoenix, we have several main clients for which we design and manage their websites and email marketing campaigns. In my time in the web department, I have designed many emails for these clients whose needs range across a broad spectrum of styles and markets. This includes everything from graphical promotion emails, to product marketing emails, to transactional and text emails. In addition to designing emails, I also frequently mockup changes to their websites to send send to the client to approve before the changes are implemented.

Both the email and web graphic design process involve constant feedback with the clients, incorporating their needs and desired changes throughout the process. Each email and website update has strict deadlines, so I am constantly working within timelines for multiple projects at a time. I typically use Photoshop or Fireworks to do design the emails and website changes, but I also have some light experience building/coding the emails and websites themselves in Dreamweaver.

DOLLAR CONCRETE

dollarconcrete.com


Dollar Concrete Construcion is a privately-owned commercial concrete building contractor based out of Norcross, GA. Their website had not been updated since 2006, so the goal for this project was to modernize the content, style, and functionality of the site to better direct traffic to the comapny.

THE OLD SITE


Dollar's existing site consisted of outdated information in an outdated style. There were no interactive elements, and much of the contact information was not relevant anymore. Because of this, their site was not driving much traffic, so Dollar wanted to revamp the design to give themselves a better presence online and bring in more business from the web.

REDESIGNING & REBUILDING


I met with the Dollar Concrete senior management team and discussed what changes they wanted in content, what kind of style they were looking for, and I worked with them on how best to lay it out. They provided updated images of current jobs, copy, and information about the management and services that helped me build the design to suit their needs. We held several meetings to touch base on the progression of the site, and went through a few iterations of designs and layouts along the way.

This project was the first full front-end web design job I had done, so in order to better familiarize myself with HTML and CSS along the way, I coded the site from scratch in the web-oriented coding program, Brackets. In addition to my code, I also used some open source elements for the some of the animated bits, modifying them to suit Dollar's needs. Because it was my first freelance project and I was still fairly new to coding, Dollar was very accomodating with their delivery dates, allowing me time to make sure everything was done right.

THE NEW SITE


After incorporating all of the elements discussed throughout the design process, I presented Dollar with a final local version of the site. After some finishing tweaks, the site was ready to upload and went live in summer of 2015. The new site incorporates more modernized interactive elements and up-to-date information put together in a newer, fresher style.


dollarconcrete.com

Dollar Concrete has been very pleased with the new website, and we believe that it will give them a better presence online and hopefully drive more traffic to their site. As they add new projects to their portfolio, I will be working with them to keep the site updated going forward in the future.
